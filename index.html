About Me

Francella Fiallos (she/her) is a front-end web developer who made the switch to tech after a decade in the community broadcasting and arts non-profit sectors. Trained as a journalist, she brings an analytical and inquisitive lens to fast-paced environments in order to troubleshoot, solve problems, and manage complex projects.

Outside of coding, she's a radio host, Polaris Music Prize juror, and board member for Debaser, an Ottawa/Gatineau DIY-arts organization.

Superconnected
